Angular's Approach (for comparison):
View Encapsulation: Angular components, by default, use "View Encapsulation"
 (often Shadow DOM emulation) to encapsulate their styles.
  This means that styles defined within a component's CSS file are generally scoped 
  to that component and do not "leak" out to affect other components,
   including its children,
    unless explicitly overridden (e.g., using ::ng-deep, which is deprecated).
